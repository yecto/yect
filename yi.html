allée de yi'

Hi FARR, I would like to have the pleasure of recieving your help to create 'allée de yi'', a non conventional book that puts together diverse sound and visual explorations created through live coding. The nonbook will have the shape of a small paper box, that will contains an audio cassette with 5 songs and a zine with 5 visual explorations, both sharing the results of live processes that cames from an algorithmic/digital materiality, created from software that allows to improvise wrting code, and evaluating it on the fly; this live coded materiality will migrate, becoming an object.

Through 'allée' I want to invite you to reinterpret, explore and feel the algorithm far from the screen. Also, exist a nostalgic love for the transit from the digital processes to the printing machine.

'allée de yi'' signifies 'ligth alley', a mix between words in French, Spanish and Zapoteco. From south to north, in all the Earth planet, the human always was being curious about the universe. Exist different poetic and ancestral cosmic interpretations, some of them are maps or different kind of writings; these are the inspiration for 'alleé de yi'', that will contains 5 different cosmic imaginary abstract shapes, sounds and signs in movement.



/ / / / / / / / / / Developments ~ Visual explorations for allée de yi'. / /

Is also important to say that I will create all the visual explorations through a DIY interface that a friend and me wrote, it is called 's2hs2' and works on top of two different open source softwares Processing and TidalCycles.

Live coding is influenced by FLOSS (Free Libre Open Source Software) philosophy, and personally I would love to share all the algorithms involved in the creation of the book through a web page, as a web publication with photos, videos, sounds and code with the idea to open the processes and invite all to rewrite it.
