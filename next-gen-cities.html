CERC, Next-Generation Cities / Data Visualization




I've been invited to collaborate, creating an interactive map of the 'CERC' (Canada Excellence Research Chair in Smart, Sustainable and Resilient Communities and Cities), Next-Generation Cities, 'a world leader in the development of urban transformation strategies, the implementation of renewable energy and resource efficiency measures in cities, and the performance optimization of municipal districts'.

The project was presented as part of a public event in the 4th space at Concordia University, the map was created through the JavaScript library d3.js and displayed on a touch screen monitor, allowing interested people to navigate through the concepts and relations between the various research interests of the cluster members.
