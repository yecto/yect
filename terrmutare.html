Terrmutare is a collaborative project between Rodrigo Torres, Malitzin Cortés, César García and Rodrigo Velasco, it took the form of an installation presented in Casa Galería, Mexico City, 2016. The approach of this project is that the people who inhabit a territory transform it with their movement. We suggest to 'dérive' (drift) an interactive installation inside of an abandoned house full of doorframes. I had the pleasure of create the audioreactive generative visuals that represent a mutant territory, they where connected to proximity sensors that activates different states of the visuals and turn off or on diverse lights, every time a person crossed a door.
