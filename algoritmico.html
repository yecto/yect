Algo Rítmico, ELECTROLIVE


All rythms & most synths in this album were live coded during the production process. We sampled tracks from our Winter 2016 Solstice annual giga-improv where live coder Jason Levine (New-York) meets Marc Egg Matatya (Montréal) with special collaboration by yecto (Ecatepec) & slam poets Arturo Waldo (Mexico, DF), & Ab Rah (Montaren-et-Saint-Mediers).

Mix & Production were made by Vieux Neant @ Studio House of Cards, Montréal 2016 / 2017.


* vocals_francais_Ab Rah / Code Rythm_Jason Levine / guitar & vocals espanol_Arturo Waldo / code rhythm & vocal espanol_yecto / rhodes, synths & english vocals_Marc Egg Matatya / Bass_walk in _stranger / mix & prod_Vieux Neant.
