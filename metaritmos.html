Metaritmos

Vieux Néant, Likwid Trees & yecto




As 'Metaritmos', Charles Bicari, Marc Matatya & Rodrigo Velasco create improv-based audiovisual experiences blending synthesis, live coding, sequences, voice and audio-reactive visuals. Metaritmos is a hybrid game of live improvisation influenced by traditional polyrhythms and contemporary synthesis.

Metaritmos originally refers to jam sessions and performances across Montréal and Mexico City including sequencers, synthesisers, live coding and visuals. These events serve as a lab within which various practices in live electronics interact.

* The previous video contains collaborations that gave way to 'Metaritmos' through Algoraves and jam sessions occurred in Montréal around 2017.
