Live coding en 'Nuit Blanche Noche en Blanco Latinx (Re)mix'

NVA x EASTERN BLOC, MONTRÉAL - 2018 -

Rodrigo Velasco a présenté un ensemble audiovisuel de live coding dans le cadre de 'Noche en Blanco Latinx (Re) mix' organisé par Eastern Bloc et Never Apart.



Noche en blanco: Latinx (Re)mix celebrated the many facets of diasporic latinx creative identities and beyond while including a series of media installations and high-level musical performances.

This event took place March 3rd, 2018 at Eastern Bloc.

Relisten to the various takes on latinx musics, from live coded sonic landscapes, latin trap, rave music and relentless body-moving percussion.
