ELO 2018, Montréal





alphautre is an ongoing exploration at the crossroads of live coding and electronic literature, I had the opportunity to present a first iteration 'alphautre : maíz' that include drawings by @malditoperrito. The audiovisual set was presented at the BlackBox - Concordia University as part of ELO 2018. This was only possible thanks to the invitation and support of the digital writings laboratory 'TOPO'. Through their proposal 'Présence Mexicaine à ELO 2018', I did an artist residency in the Mile End before the conference and presented these explorations as an improvised performance through s2hs2 <
