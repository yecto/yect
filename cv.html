CV - Rodrigo Iván Ramírez Velasco


Collaborations / 2017 - ‘Metarythmes’ is a series of meetings between TOPLAP, OBORO and Studio XX, Rodrigo Velasco + Charles Bicari, Montréal / 2015 - Creation of ‘s2hs2’ an interface to live code text, visuals and design, Alex McLean + Rodrigo Velasco, Leeds / 2015 - ‘onfcopoe’ - a group to explore relations between poetics and live coding, Leeds, Mexico, Montréal.


Education / 2016 - Master in Visual Arts (Digital Art), Facultad de Artes y Diseño, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México, UNAM / 2015 - Research stay in the ‘Interdisciplinary Centre for Scientific Research in Music’, University of Leeds / 2012 - Diploma in Video Digital, Facultad de Artes y Diseño, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México, UNAM / 2010 - Bachelor of Graphic Design, Universidad Tecnológica de México, UNITEC.


Imparted Workshops / 2016 - TidalCycles 'birds of a feather' Workshop - Mike Hodnick, Scott Fradkin, Alex McLean and Rodrigo Velasco at International Conference on Live Coding 2016, McMaster University, Hamilton / 2016 - ‘Live Coding in TidalCycles, s2hs2 + SuperDirt’ at interactive.do / 2014 - ‘Live Coding introduction through TidalCycles’ - Facultad de Música, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México.


Experience / 2015 - Founder, web and graphic designer, ‘veería’, Mexico, Montréal, Leeds. / 2016 - Creative coding with Processing and frontend web for ‘interactive.do’ / 2015 - Web Programming and Digital Design Professor, ‘Universidad del Valle de México’, UVM / 2013 - Graphic and New Media Design Professor, ’Universidad Doctor Emilio Cárdenas’, UDEC / 2014 - Creative Director as part of ‘E-Learning Improve S.C’ / 2011 - Multimedia Designer as part of ‘Televisión Educativa’, Secretaria de Educación Pública.


Achievements / 2017 - ‘Astr. immersive live coding', selected as emergent artist in Labsessions #12 organized by la Société des arts technologiques (SAT) in Montréal / 2017 - Visual identity thorugh live coding for ‘ICLC 2017‘ - International Conference on Live Coding, Morelia, Mexique / 2017 - TR-808 in Mixmag - Welcome to the Algorave movement, mixed by Yaxu.

Espectacles / 2017 - I organized and performed as «yecto», in the two Algoraves in Montréal, at Casa del Popolo and La Plante / 2016 - «yecto» at Algorave, ICLC 2016 in Hamilton, Canada / 2014 - «otú» Simposio Internacional de Música y Código /* vivo */ 2014 / 2014 - «otú» at Network Music Festival, Brighton, UK.
