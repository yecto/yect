s2hs2

s2hs2 is an interface between TidalCycles and Processing. A visual form can be assigned to each letter on the computer keyboard, visualising patterns of words (visual forms) live coded from Tidal. s2hs2 is a collaborative project undertaken by Rodrigo Velasco and Alex McLean in January 2015 in the School of Music, University of Leeds.
