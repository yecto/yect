Bez tig


Bez tig is a collaboration between Montréal based, Mexican artists Oscar Coyoli and Rodrigo Velasco. Bez tig means tiger in Zapotec, an intelligent and agile feline that the autochthonous from Oaxaca blessed. This particular strength, balance and lightness of the animal is reflected in the energetic hybrid of nostalgic soundscapes, vocal explorations, afro-latin live coded rhythms and audio-reactive 3D visuals that enable the emergence of a light celebration.


Sound is improvised through an algorithmic-analog dialogue between relentless body-moving percussion, vocal free experimentation, meditative atmospheres and singing. In relation to visuals, Velasco and Coyoli are overlaying 3D shapes that they’ve created, live coding, folding and unfolding them through ese2.
