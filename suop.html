Suöp!


Improvising at the thresholds of algorithmic-analog topographies, creating an space for immanent play. A kind of kaleidoscopic topography will be created, as projected imagery and sound splash, slip and curve through a multiplicity of refracting textures and shadows in space. The emergent space will be continually shifted and collaged with materials (such as bubble paper, tubes, springs, mirrors, plants, shadows) and the projection of audio-visual live coding (TidalCycles, Hydra, Fluxus) beside the drift of generative texts and interactive visuals in the web browser.

The sudden, the surprise, the ‘oops!’ and flux of the code will extend into the equally live and improvisational movements of materials in the space. This immanent and co-compositional field of experience will reverberate with ghostly textures and polyrhythms, offering a multiplicity of thresholds through which to explore and celebrate coding as an event, ‘as it happens’.
